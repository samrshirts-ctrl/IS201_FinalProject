    My favorite fish and why:

  1. Yellowfin Tuna

    • I think they look pretty
    • They are my favorite fish to draw
    • Although bluefin tuna are generally higher quality for food, I don't like eating fish, so that doesn't matter to me

  2. Mola Mola (giant ocean sunfish)

    • They're not very good at being fish, and that amuses me
    • They eat nothing but jellyfish, and their only defense against predators is their lack of nutritional value
    • The only reason they aren't extinct is because they have the highest reproduction rate of any creature

  3. Rainbow Trout

    • They are the only type of fish I have ever fished
    • They have beautiful colors
    • I like watching them swim together as a school of fish
